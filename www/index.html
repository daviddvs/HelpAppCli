Accident - Traffic

What can I do?

  1. Check yourself for injuries
  2. Check on the well-being of yours passengers
  3. Get to safety
  4. Call 112
  5. Wait for help
  6. Exchange information

Stolen/Lost Goods - Papers

What can I do?

  1. Act fast → The first thing to do is to make sure that your papers are really missing and that you did not simply place them somewhere else. In case missing them, make a checklist of what you have missed.
  2. Contact your embassy → Then, you must contact your embassy and explain your situation.
  3. Make a police report → Head to the nearest police station and file a report. You will need to do this specially if you have lost your credit as well or need to make insurance claims.
  4. Head to your embassy → Your embassy will help issue your temporary passport, help you with arrangements and also advice on proper procedure. Note that you might need to pay for the issuance of a new passport, unless in certain circumstances where the fee may be waived.

Where can I go?

Violence - Rape

What can I do?

The first thing you have to do when you have been raped, is ask for help. There is specific number for those emergencies; 016. There, they will give you all the information you need to know. They will sugest you to go to the hospital for proper health examination. Once you have been examined, you will have to put a claim in the police station.

Where can I go?

When you call to 016, they will explain you all you have to know. They will send you all the help you need in any case.

Violence - Brawl

What can I do?

It doesn´t matter if you are involved or not in the fight, you have to call the emergency services. They will redirect you to the police. In case there is someone hurt, you can also ask for an ambulance. If it is necessary, you can put a claim later.

Where can I go?

If there isn´t anyone hurt and the fight is over, you can also go to the nearest police station, or stay where you are waiting for emergency services.

Fire - Building

What can I do?

First of all, you have to evaluate if you are ask to handle the fire. Every building must have an extinguisher, so you can take and use it. If needed, each extinguisher has the instructions of how to use it. Then, activate the fire alarm. Each building has fire exits, use them to leave the building.

Once you are save you can call the emergency services. They will evaluate the situation and send the help necessary in any case.

Where can I go?

Go to the nearest police station if you feel okey, if not, go to the hospital
